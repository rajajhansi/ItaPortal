Title: "iLamaiyil kal" puththaga kuzhu

Duration: Offered two Sundays - 11:15 to 12.

Person to Contact: Loganathan Venkatachalam (408) 984 8355

Email: loguv@hotmail.com

Reading is an essential part of knowledge enrichment. The book club will encourage and motivate reading Tamil literature and story books that will help to develop students' knowledge of Tamil language, Fluent reading skill and culture.

Goal:

Enjoy Tamil through reading and listening to Tamil stories
Develop Tamil literary knowledge through discussion of several topics

Program detail:

the club will meet monthly twice from 11:15 to 12.

the club moderator will read a book and discuss the subject in Tamil (alternate weeks)

Suggest a topic for the next meeting and encourage students to borrow books on that subject to read at home with parents if needed

parents are welcome to the club to read with the kids - (alternate weeks) students will be given opportunity to come and tell all about the book he/she read the prior week. The format of the discussion would be as follows:

name of the book
what kind of book
author
what they like or don't like about the book
they would recommend the book? why or why not?
Books:

The books will include but not limited to
stories by kids writers that enable critical thinking
children's songs
Tamil magazines:Chutti Vikatan, Thendral, SiruvarMani etc
books written by great Tamil authors
humorous books
fairy tales
Folklore stories - etc

Competition:

No competition at this point. The objective of this club is to encourage students reading habits through fun books. I don't plan to have any competition, tests or ranking students. Not but not least: This agenda will be enhanced as we learn more about the members and their interest.
