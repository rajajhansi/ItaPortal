Process of the application and submission:

All payments due to the University towards Registration, Admission and Certificate etc., are to be remitted in a forum of a check written in favor of California Tamil Academy.

The check should be mailed to the following address:

California Tamil Academy
20532, Deerpark CT
Saratoga, CA 95070
USA

FEES ONCE PAID WILL NOT BE REFUNDED UNDER ANY CIRCUMSTANCE.

Tuition fees structure for the courses is given in our site separately.

Need to pay the course fees and application fee

Application fee (UG- $10/ PG - $20/ MBA - $50)

Examination fees will be collected during the examination

(i) Name of the student, programme and enrollment number should be written on a paper and attach it to the check.
(ii) The check should be accompanied by the remittance slip, duly filled in. The remittance slip formats available in the prospectus. The student can use the Xerox copy of the same.
(iii) The tuition fee and other fee due for the first year are to be paid immediately on receipt of the Admission intimation and before the last date fixed in the intimation letter. No extension of time for the payment of fee will be allowed. For payment of Tuition fee and other fee for the subsequent year, no separate intimation will be sent. The candidates must, on their own, arrange to remit the fee by end of July.

Study of the Programme

The study of the programme, syllabi, scheme of examinations, evaluation, passing minimum, classification, rules and regulations, etc., will be the same as per regular students admitted to the colleges affiliated to this University.

General Instructions

(i) The method of instruction is through

a) lesson materials and
b) personal contact programmes (PCP)
The lesson materials shall be supplied to the candidates.
PCP centres. Every effort will be made to hand over the lesson materials for each paper during contact programmes. Students are advised to check the lessons received by them.

(ii) Purchase of prescribed text books, if any, is compulsory.

(iii)  All communications / notification will be sent to the students in advance by post under certificate of posting. However there is every possibility of delay / loss of letters. Hence, candidates are instructed to check / verify then and there the communications / notifications received by them with their classmates, so that they will not be missing any of the communications. CDE will not take any responsibility for postal delay / loss in transit.

iv) The CDE is not responsible for any delay in sending lesson materials, communications, etc., if the payment has been made after the due date prescribed.

v) All disputes are subjected to Trichy Jurisdiction only.
