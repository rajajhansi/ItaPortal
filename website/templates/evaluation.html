Rules and regulations for evaluation:

1. New Student Evaluation:

  • There is No Evaluation Test for new students registering in Preschool and Basic-I classes.

  • All the new students aged 6 years and above are evaluated for their Tamil skill when they register in CTA or its Affiliates.

  • If your child is registering first time to CTA and is above 5 years, please provide the information about your child to help us place your child in the right grade.
    Click here to download the Evaluation Form

  • The students will be placed in the appropriate grade level based on their evaluation test scores .

  • Students must score 80 and above to qualify for the grade.

2. Evaluation of Returning Students:

  • Returning students can take the evaluation test to skip a grade as long as they meet the minimum age requirements.

  • The student has to pass the evaluation test with a score of 80 & above to be promoted.

  • Returning students will be evaluated ONLY on the first 3 working days of the school.

3. Grade Eligibility:

In order for the student to be evaluated for a grade the student has to satisfy the age requirements as listed below.

Preschool 1 - 3 Years
Preschool 2 - 4 Years
Basic 1 - 5 Years
Basic 2 - 6 Years
Grade 1 - 7 Years
Grade 2 - 8 Years
Grade 3 - 9 Years
Grade 4 - 10 Years
Grade 5 - 11 Years
Grade 6 - 12 Years
Grade 7 - 13 Years
